Off The Grid Donut Shop

This is a simple visual representation of donut sales throughout the day. Now, a poem.
I turned around the corner
walked down the block
walked right in to a donut shop
pulled two donuts right out of the grease
handed the lady a five cent piece
she looked at the nickel and looked at me
said "this nickel's no good to me"
"there's a hole in the nickel and that won't do!"
I said "Aye! Theres a hole in a donut too!"
